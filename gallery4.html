What is Professional Whitening?

Advanced clinical treatment for a bright, confident smile

Professional teeth whitening is a highly effective cosmetic procedure that uses medical-grade whitening agents to break down deep-seated stains. Unlike over-the-counter kits, our clinical treatment is faster, safer, and produces more uniform results.

Hydrogen Peroxide Gel

LED Light Activation

Common Staining Causes

We can help reverse discoloration caused by various factors

Food & Drink

Coffee, tea, red wine, and dark-colored berries

Tobacco Use

Tar and nicotine create stubborn yellowish/brown stains

Aging

Natural wearing of enamel revealing the yellower dentin beneath

Medication

Staining caused by certain antibiotics or fluorosis

Poor Hygiene

Inadequate brushing leading to plaque-based discoloration

Special Occasions

Weddings or interviews where you want to look your best

Post-Whitening Care

Maximize the life of your pearly whites

White Diet

Avoid colored foods (coffee, turmeric, berries) for the first 48 hours.

Hydration

Drink plenty of water to help keep your enamel hydrated and healthy.

Touch-Up Kits

Use our recommended home kits for minor maintenance every few months.

Quit Smoking

Avoid tobacco to prevent immediate surface re-staining.

Pro Tip

Drinking through a straw helps bypass the front teeth and prevents future staining!
