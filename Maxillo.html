Types of Maxillofacial Prostheses

Specialized prosthetic solutions for facial reconstruction

Ocular Prosthesis

Artificial eye replacement

  • Custom-painted iris matching
  • Realistic sclera coloring
  • Proper eyelid movement
  • Regular polishing & maintenance
For: Eye socket defects
Most Complex

Nasal Prosthesis

Artificial nose reconstruction

  • Anatomically accurate shape
  • Skin tone matching
  • Breathing function restoration
  • Adhesive or implant retained
For: Nasal defects

Auricular Prosthesis

Artificial ear replacement

  • Mirror opposite ear symmetry
  • Realistic cartilage details
  • Helix and antihelix definition
  • Implant or adhesive fixation
For: Microtia or trauma

Maxillofacial Prosthesis Process

Comprehensive approach to facial rehabilitation

1

Initial Consultation & Assessment

Multidisciplinary evaluation involving prosthodontist, surgeon, and patient to understand needs, expectations, and surgical site condition.

60-90 mins
2

Impression & Records

Detailed impressions of the defect area, photographs, measurements, and shade matching of adjacent skin tones.

45-60 mins
3

Wax Try-In & Sculpting

Creation of wax prototype for fitting, shape verification, and patient feedback on aesthetics and comfort.

60 mins
4

Final Prosthesis Fabrication

Processing of medical-grade silicone with custom coloring, texture matching, and intrinsic/extrinsic coloration.

2-3 weeks lab time
5

Delivery & Follow-up Care

Final fitting, adhesion instruction, maintenance training, and scheduled follow-up appointments for adjustments.

60 mins

Benefits of Maxillofacial Prosthesis

Comprehensive rehabilitation and quality of life improvement

Social Integration

Restores facial appearance allowing normal social interactions and reducing stigma associated with facial differences.

Psychological Well-being

Significantly improves self-esteem, body image, and overall psychological health after traumatic loss or surgery.

Functional Restoration

Improves speech clarity, swallowing ability, chewing function, and breathing depending on the defect location.

Wound Protection

Protects surgical sites or defects from environmental contaminants, temperature extremes, and mechanical trauma.

Aesthetic Customization

Highly customizable to match individual skin tones, textures, and facial features with lifelike realism.

Non-Surgical Option

Provides facial reconstruction without additional surgical procedures for patients who cannot undergo further surgery.
