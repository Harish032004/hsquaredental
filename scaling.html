Types of Scaling

Advanced cleaning techniques for different dental needs

Manual Scaling

Traditional method using specialized dental instruments

  • Precision cleaning
  • No electricity needed
  • Gentle on teeth
  • Affordable option
Best For: Mild Tartar
Most Popular

Ultrasonic Scaling

Advanced cleaning with high-frequency vibrations

  • Faster cleaning
  • Less discomfort
  • Water irrigation
  • Deep plaque removal
Best For: Heavy Tartar

Air Polishing

Gentle stain removal with air, water & powder

  • Stain removal
  • No vibration
  • Smoother surface
  • Tobacco/coffee stains
Best For: Surface Stains

Scaling Procedure

Step-by-step professional cleaning process

1

Initial Examination

Complete oral examination to assess plaque and tartar buildup.

10-15 mins
2

Supragingival Cleaning

Removal of plaque and tartar above the gum line.

15-20 mins
3

Subgingival Cleaning

Deep cleaning below gum line in periodontal pockets.

10-15 mins
4

Root Planing

Smoothing tooth roots to prevent bacterial buildup.

10-15 mins
5

Polishing & Fluoride

Final polishing and fluoride treatment for protection.

5-10 mins

Benefits of Scaling

Why regular professional cleaning is essential

Prevent Gum Disease

Removes bacteria that cause gingivitis and periodontitis.

Fresh Breath

Eliminates bad breath caused by bacterial buildup.

Brighter Smile

Removes stains from coffee, tea, tobacco, and food.

Overall Health

Reduces risk of heart disease, diabetes complications.

Cost Saving

Prevents expensive dental treatments in future.

Cavity Prevention

Removes plaque that causes tooth decay and cavities.

Aftercare & Maintenance

Ensure long-lasting results with proper care

First 24 Hours

Avoid hot foods/drinks. No brushing for 4-6 hours. Rinse with salt water.

Daily Oral Care

Brush twice daily with soft brush. Floss regularly. Use antiseptic mouthwash.

Regular Checkups

Visit dentist every 6 months. Professional cleaning recommended twice a year.
