What are Clear Aligners?

Modern dental technology for a straighter, healthier smile

Clear aligners are an advanced alternative to traditional braces. They use a series of custom-made, clear plastic trays to apply gentle pressure and move your teeth into alignment without any wires or brackets.

Medical Grade Plastic

AI-Powered Planning

Hygiene Friendly

DisComfort Comfort and Invisible

Who Needs Clear Aligners?

Correct various alignment issues comfortably

Crowded Teeth

When there isn't enough space in the jaw for all teeth to fit normally

Gap Teeth

Closing extra spaces between one or more teeth

Overbite

When upper teeth overlap significantly over lower teeth

Crossbite

When both upper and lower jaws are misaligned

Relapse Case

Correcting teeth that shifted after previous braces treatment

Aesthetic Fixing

Minor adjustments for a more symmetrical and confident smile

The Aligner Journey

Your path to a perfect smile in 5 simple steps

1

3D Digital Scan

No messy molds! We take a precise 3D digital scan of your teeth to create a virtual treatment plan.

2

Treatment Preview

See a 3D animation of how your teeth will move and how your final smile will look before starting.

3

Custom Sets

Receive your series of custom-made clear aligners, usually changing sets every 1-2 weeks.

4

Wear & Move

Wear aligners for 20-22 hours a day. Take them out only to eat, drink, and clean your teeth.

5

Retention Phase

Once your teeth are straight, we provide retainers to keep your new smile in place permanently.

Why Choose Clear Aligners?

The clear advantage over traditional metal braces

Discreet Look

Most people won't even notice you're wearing them because they're transparent.

Eat Anything

No food restrictions! Just remove them to eat your favorite crunchy or sticky foods.

Superior Comfort

Smooth plastic means no sharp wires or brackets to poke your cheeks and gums.

Better Oral Health

Brushing and flossing is normal, reducing the risk of plaque and gum disease.

Fewer Visits

Fewer office visits needed compared to traditional braces adjustments.

Faster Results

In many cases, clear aligners can straighten teeth faster than traditional methods.

Aligner Aftercare

Keep your aligners clear and your smile moving

Daily Cleaning

Rinse your aligners every time you remove them. Clean with a soft brush.

Only Water

Do not drink colored beverages with aligners on to avoid staining.

The Case Rule

If they aren't in your mouth, they're in the case. Never wrap in tissues.

22-Hour Rule

Commit to wearing them 22 hours daily for the most efficient results.

Aligner Tip

Always brush your teeth after eating before putting your aligners back in!
