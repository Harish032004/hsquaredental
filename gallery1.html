What are Porcelain Veneers?

Thin ceramic shells that transform your smile completely

Porcelain veneers are custom-made, ultra-thin shells of tooth-colored ceramic material designed to cover the front surface of teeth. They are permanently bonded to your teeth, changing their color, shape, size, or length.

0.3 - 0.5 mm Thickness

Who Needs Porcelain Veneers?

Solve multiple dental issues with one solution

Discolored Teeth

Teeth stained by tetracycline, fluoride, or large resin fillings

Worn Down Teeth

Teeth that are worn down, chipped, or broken

Misaligned Teeth

Teeth that are crooked, uneven, or irregularly shaped

Gaps Between Teeth

Close spaces or gaps between teeth (diastema)

Worn Enamel

Teeth with worn enamel causing sensitivity

Benefits of Porcelain Veneers

Why choose porcelain veneers for your smile transformation

Natural Appearance

Mimics light reflection properties of natural teeth, creating a completely natural look.

Stain Resistant

Porcelain surface resists coffee, tea, wine, and tobacco stains better than natural teeth.

Gum Compatibility

Porcelain is biocompatible and well-tolerated by gum tissues.

Color Customization

Can be made brighter than your natural teeth for a whitening effect.

Durability

With proper care, porcelain veneers can last 10-15 years or more.

Quick Transformation

Dramatic smile makeover in just 2-3 visits to our clinic.

Caring for Your Veneers

Maintain your perfect smile for years to come

Oral Hygiene

Brush twice daily with non-abrasive toothpaste. Floss regularly.

Dietary Habits

Avoid biting hard foods/objects. Limit stain-causing foods and drinks.

Regular Checkups

Visit us every 6 months for professional cleaning and examination.

Protection

Wear a night guard if you grind teeth. Use mouthguard during sports.

Pro Tip

Veneers require the same care as natural teeth. No special maintenance needed!
