Our Crown & Bridge Procedure

Step-by-step process for perfect permanent restorations

1

Consultation & Preparation

Examination, X-rays, and preparation of the tooth structure for crown placement.

60-90 mins
2

Impressions & Shade Selection

Precise digital or traditional impressions and matching your natural tooth color.

30-45 mins
3

Temporary Crown Placement

Fitting a temporary crown to protect the prepared tooth while lab crafts permanent one.

30 mins
4

Final Crown Fitting

Removing temporary crown and cementing the custom-made permanent crown/bridge.

60 mins
5

Adjustment & Aftercare

Bite adjustment, polishing, and guidance on maintenance for long-lasting results.

30 mins

Benefits of Dental Crowns & Bridges

Experience restored dental function and confidence

Tooth Protection

Encapsulates and protects weakened, cracked, or extensively filled teeth from further damage.

Restored Chewing

Regain full chewing function with strong, durable crowns that withstand normal biting forces.

Aesthetic Improvement

Custom-colored restorations that match your natural teeth, enhancing your smile appearance.

Prevents Shifting

Bridges prevent adjacent teeth from shifting into the gap, maintaining proper dental alignment.

Long-Term Solution

Durable restorations that last 15+ years with proper care, providing excellent value.

Speech Clarity

Properly fitted crowns restore normal tooth contours, improving speech and pronunciation.
